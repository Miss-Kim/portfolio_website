Case Study ⌈GRO⌉

Overview

So,What Exactly is GRO?

GRO is a responsive e-wallet app that allows users to quickly and safely store their bank information and credit card information in one place, enabling users to shop online and in-store(touch feature). Aslo, give users the freedom to split bills with others, send and request money with those who don't use the same e-wallet.

Discovery Phase

PROBLEM STATEMENT

E-wallet app needs a new way to securely store multiple cards,and bank information, whilst keeping track of all financial transactions and expenses.
Also, allows users to safely "transfer and request" money to other who does not use the same e-wallet app.

SOLUTIONS

Create a universal e-wallet that can communicate with other apps, also allow users to store multiple bank information in one place.
This way, we can offer users better control of their finance, freedom to perform online transactions with other receivers without having to use multiple e-wallet and perform touchless in-store payments.

Discovery and Research

WHO

Age between 18 - 40, young and busy professional, looking for a universal e-wallet that would help save time and money while security is the greatest concern.


WHERE

Primary use will mostly be on mobile phone, on the go.


WHEN

Anytime a user needs an online checkout or in-store checkout.
Reminder notification even when you are not using the app.


WHAT

Empower users to be more aware of their spending habits, gain more control of their finance and offer reminders to stay on top of their budget.


WHY

Save time, save money, never late payment fees, easy to follow instructions, touchless and financial freedom.


HOW

Give suggestions to save up, notify when bills are soon to due, easy to follow purchased history avaliable to users with diagrams/ charts.
