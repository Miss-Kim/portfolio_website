My Story

Hello, I am Kim. I am a passionate UX & UI Designer and a cat enthusiast - hoping to further my career in the design space.

My journey into UX started when I wanted a career change in early 2021. My previous work experience in retail and healthcare lead me to find the common thread in UX as I want to understand people so that I can improve their lives in some way. I am currently learning Front-end Development for Designers - so that I will be able to create effective design systems and handoff assets the correct way and collaborate better with the devs.

I was born in Myanmar but immigrated to Oslo with my family in 2005. Since moving to Oslo, I have published 2 books and have been featured in another 2 books, founded a non-profit Humanitarian Aid Organization in 2012 - to help and provide orphanages in Myanmar and successfully completed 6 projects for 4860 children. Today I am a co-founder of 4Gladiators indie gaming studio where I help create content for the games to bug testing. My roles shaped my process-oriented approach to problem-solving along with a genuine curiosity to learn.

I describe myself as a wanderlust. My passion for travel and culture has led me to make connections and friendships in 22 countries and learn languages as I speak 3, and I am learning Japanese at the moment.

Thanks for taking this time to get to know me. If you're so inclined, I'd like to get to know you too! Just fill out the form at the bottom. If you want to talk about design or have a project you want to share with me? Connect with me. Let's talk!
