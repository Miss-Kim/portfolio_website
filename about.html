My Story

I began my career with a 10+ years of experience in retail and healthcare; client acquisition has taught me how to identify customer pain points and present solutions that work for clients. I am armed with a wide array of skills from diffierent fields from NGO work in Burma to concept creation, in-game designing, testing, and user researching with 4Gladiators game company. In the beginning of 2021, I decided to make a career change. I took up UX Design bootcamp with CAREERFOUNDRY where I create user personas, moodboards, create mockups and then create high fidelity end-to-end product with Figma as my main tool. After 10 months of generalized design experience, I decided to focus in on frontend webdevelopment design, sharpening my experience with a certificate in UX design.

I believe beautiful products can also be intuitive, and love coming up with different solutions for processes. My experience with frontend means I can easily communicate with developers, and I design around the constraints of coding. I have a demonstrated talent for creating beautiful designs. I like to take the initiative, pick up new skills, and take on new challenges. I am an extremely fast learner, familiar with Agile workflow, and approach design with a high degree of empathy for both the user and the developer.
