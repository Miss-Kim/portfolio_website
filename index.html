Case Study ⌈GRO⌉

GRO

So, What Exactly is GRO?

GRO is a responsive e-wallet app that allows users to quickly and safely store their bank information and credit card information in one place, enabling users to shop online and in-store(touchless feature). Also, give users the freedom to split bills with others, send and request money with those who don't use the same e-wallet.
